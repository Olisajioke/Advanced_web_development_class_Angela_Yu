Optometrists Vanguard

We are a community of Nigerian optometrists living and practicing in Saudi Arabia. Our platform was created to make the journey of settling into the Kingdom easier, friendlier, and more rewarding for fellow colleagues.Here you’ll find practical advice, shared experiences, and resources to help you navigate both professional and everyday life. From learning simple Arabic phrases for smoother communication, to tips on adapting to local culture, we make sure no one feels alone in the process. We also provide spaces to connect: a job board for new opportunities, and a small marketplace where members can buy and sell equipment or household items — especially useful when moving between cities within the Kingdom. At the heart of it all, this is a family-friendly hub where older colleagues pass down wisdom, newer colleagues find support, and together we grow into a stronger, thriving network of Nigerian optometrists in Saudi Arabia.
